    • Our Mission

    Our mission at the New Christian Order is to create a network of Christians dedicated to building the kingdom and lifting each other up. NCO is a fraternal organization based on the lodge system. We provide benefits to our members like networking events, expert-led seminars, access to interest-free loans, and of course, brotherhood.


    We currently have lodges in Ausitn, Texas; Bend, Oregon; and Wilkes-barre, Pennsylvania. If you are interested in joining (even if you live in a different area), sign up on our Join page and one of our head marshalls will reach out to you.
