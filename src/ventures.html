    • Our Business Ventures

    The Lodge Representatives and NCO Executives work together to create and manage nonprofit and commercial ventures that further our mission. Official members of NCO are obligated to contribute in a meaningful way to at least one or more ventures.

    Nonprofits

    Public Outreach Initiative
    POI is a community action nonprofit focused on faith-based activism, and supporting members to make changes in their communities and local governments.
    Website: publicoutreachinitiative.org
    Jon's Blessings
    Jon's Blessings Incorporated coordinates clothing giveaways and food drives for the unhouses population of Oregon, as well as raising money for homeless shelters across the country.
    Website: jonsblessings.org

    Networking

    Christian-American Business Club
    The public-facing branch of the NCO, CABC works to connect Christian business owners through expert-led seminars and networking dinners hosted by Lodge Marshalls at our various chapters across the country.
    Visit the directory page for more information
