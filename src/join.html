    Welcome to the Christian-American Business Owners' Fraternity, where we foster spiritual discernment through brotherhood and the Holy Spirit for the success of our members. Our lodge meetings consist of prayer for members' endeavors, discussion of business opportunities among members, Bible study led by the lodge marshal, and coordination of charity and social events for the fraternity.

    Requirements for Joining:

    • Candidates must be Christian.
    • Candidates must have lived in America for their entire lives.
    • Candidates must own a business or be an executive member or voting partner in a corporation or other type of organization.
    • Candidates must be in good standing with their community, religious or civic.
